OnePlus ARB Checker is a utility designed for OnePlus enthusiasts and power users who want to keep their devices safe from unintended Anti-Rollback (ARB) increases.

Features:
- Instant ARB status check for your current firmware.
- Comprehensive firmware history database for supported OnePlus models.
- Visual indicators (Safe/Fused) based on real-world data.
- Background monitoring with notifications for risky system updates.
- Material 3 design with Dynamic Color support.
- Fully private and open-source.

Stay safe while updating your OnePlus device!
